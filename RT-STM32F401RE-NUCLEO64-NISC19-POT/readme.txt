*** About ***
This demo runs on STM32 Nucleo-64 F401RE. This is a port of the USB-CDC demo
on the STM32 Nucleo. In this case shell prints on UART2 instead of USB.

*** Related article ***
This demo comes as standalone, there is no article associated with it.

*** ChibiOS related version ***
Tested under ChibiOS 19.1.0
